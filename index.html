About Me

I am a Computer Science graduate specializing in Software Engineering from the University of Wollongong in Dubai (UOWD), where I graduated with distinction. During my studies, I deliberately broadened my technical background by taking electives in cybersecurity, game development, big data, and artificial intelligence. These areas strengthened both my problem-solving mindset and my ability to work across different domains of technology. For my graduation project, I had the opportunity to collaborate with PwC for nine months. Working alongside industry professionals gave me valuable real-world experience in project development, teamwork, and practical problem solving, and it became one of the most meaningful learning experiences of my academic journey. One of my proudest achievements was earning 3rd place at our university’s Innovation Fair, where our team presented our graduation project to judges and experts from the technology industry. I am especially passionate about artificial intelligence, game development, and mobile application development. Outside of academic and project work, I actively take online courses and explore new tools to continue expanding my skills and knowledge.
